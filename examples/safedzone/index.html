How it works

PRE-SCREENING

Quickly screen individuals for important health indicators, before they arrive

  • Customizable Covid-19 screening questionnaires
  • Collect current body temperature
  • Collect current blood-oxygen level
  • Automated evaluations and feedback
  • Supports one-time and recurring screenings

ON-SITE SCREENING

Safely screen individuals when they arrive on site

  • Integrated contactless body temperature readings
  • Integrated blood oxygen (Sp02) level readings
  • Mask detection and compliance tracking
  • Occupancy limit verification

SOCIAL DISTANCE AND CONTACT TRACING

Help individuals stay socially distant and enable automated contact tracing

  • Alert individuals when they are not maintaining safe social distance
  • Log social proximity and enable automated contact tracing to more precisely determine quarantine population
  • Review trends and monitor adherence to policies

  • Expert space design and people flow services
  • Track building sanitization schedules and compliance
